I made the Gopher based on the photos below.

Materials

Yarns:

- Blue: body and ears

- White: eyes and teeth

- Black: eyes, nose and ears

- Tan: lip, arms, legs and tail

Yarn weight is not important, but please choose the hook accordingly. I used Catona cotton from Scheepjes (which is 25 gram - 62.5 m) with a 2.5 mm hook. The end product is around 9.5cm(long)*8cm(height)*6cm(wide) big.

For stuffing:

- You can use polyfill or any other type of filling you like.

Abbreviations

Rnd = round

ch = chain

st = stitch

ss = slip stitch

sc = single crochet

hdc = half double crochet

dec = decrease by sc 2 together

inc = increase by sc 2 in one st

‘...’ = the part you need to repeat

Pattern

Body

With blue yarn, start from the top of the body to the bottom of the body

Rnd 01: start with 6 sc in magic ring. (6)

Rnd 02: ‘inc’, repeat * 6. (12)

Rnd 03: ‘sc in first 1 st, inc in next’, repeat * 6. (18)

Rnd 04: ‘sc in first 2 st, inc in next’, repeat * 6. (24)

Rnd 05: ‘sc in first 3 st, inc in next’, repeat * 6. (30)

Rnd 06: ‘sc in first 4 st, inc in next’, repeat * 6. (36)

Rnd 07: ‘sc in first 5 st, inc in next’, repeat * 6. (42)

Rnd 08: ‘sc in first 6 st, inc in next’, repeat * 6. (48)

Rnd 09-15: sc each st for 7 round. (48)

Rnd 16: ‘sc in first 6 st, dec in next’, repeat * 6. (42)

Rnd 17: ‘sc in first 5 st, dec in next’, repeat * 6. (36)

Rnd 18: sc each st. (36)

Rnd 19: ‘sc in first 5 st, inc in next’, repeat * 6. (42)

Rnd 20: ‘sc in first 6 st, inc in next’, repeat * 6. (48)

Rnd 21-28: sc each st for 8 round. (48)

Rnd 29: ‘sc in first 6 st, dec in next’, repeat * 6. (42)

Rnd 30: ‘sc in first 5 st, dec in next’, repeat * 6. (36)

Rnd 31: ‘sc in first 4 st, dec in next’, repeat * 6. (30)

Rnd 32: ‘sc in first 3 st, dec in next’, repeat * 6. (24)

Rnd 33: ‘sc in first 2 st, dec in next’, repeat * 6. (18)

Rnd 33: ‘sc in first 1 st, dec in next’, repeat * 6. (12)

Rnd 33: dec all around, then fasten off and leaving a long tail for sew. (6)

Ears

With black yarn, make 2

Rnd 01: ch2, turn

Rnd 02: sc 3 times in the second st (3), then fasten off and leaving a long tail for sew

With blue yarn, make 2

Rnd 01: ch2, turn

Rnd 02: sc 3 times in the second st (3), ch1 and turn

Rnd 03: ‘inc’, repeat * 3 (6), ch1 and turn

Rnd 04: sc 1, inc, hdc 1, inc (hdc 2 in one st), sc 1 and inc (9), then fasten off and leaving a long tail for sew

Sew the black part to the blue part, and then sew the ear to the head of the body

Eyes

With black yarn, make 2

Rnd 01: start with 6 sc in magic ring(6), then fasten off and leaving a long tail for sew

With white yarn, make 2

Rnd 01: start with 6 sc in magic ring. (6)

Rnd 02: ‘inc’, repeat * 6. (12)

Rnd 03: ‘sc in first 1 st, inc in next’, repeat * 6. (18)

Rnd 04: sc each st (18), then fasten off and leaving a long tail for sew

Sew the black part to the central of the white part.

When sew the eyes to the head, slightly stuff the eyes.

Nose

With black yarn

Rnd 01: ch4, turn

Rnd 02: start form the second st, inc, sc and inc

Rnd 03: continute crochet on the other side of the chain from first round, inc, sc and inc, then fasten off and leaving a long tail for sew

Lip

With tan yarn

Rnd 01: ch5, turn

Rnd 02: start form the second st, inc, sc, sc and inc

Rnd 03: continute crochet on the other side of the chain from first round, inc, sc, sc and inc, then fasten off and leaving a long tail for sew

Teeth

With white yarn

Rnd 01: ch3, turn

Rnd 02: start form the second st, sc in each st (2), ch1 and turn

Rnd 03: sc in each st (2), then fasten off and leaving a long tail for sew

Sew the teeth, lip and nose together, then sew the whole part on the body.

Arms, Legs and Tail

With tan yarn, make 5

Rnd 01: start with 6 sc in magic ring. (6)

Rnd 02-04: sc in each st for 3 round(6), then fasten off and leaving a long tail for sew

Sew them on the body.
